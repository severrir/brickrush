How you get paid

Brick Rush runs on revenue share. Here's exactly how it works — a 30-second read before you apply.

01 · DEPARTMENTS

Departments hold the pool

Each department — Scripting, Visual (modeling + animation), and UI/UX — is allocated a share of a game's revenue.

02 · POINTS

Work earns points

Difficulty decides reward. An easy system ≈ 2 pts; a big, hard one ≈ 10 pts.

03 · PAYOUT

Points become payout

Points convert to real revenue — around 100 pts ≈ 2% of a game's revenue.

Example: as a Scripter (dept ≈ 30%), you build a hard combat system → 10 pts. Across a project you reach ~100 pts → about 2% of that game's revenue, paid from your department's share.
