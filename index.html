Revenue share

Own a piece of
what you build.

No fake promises — a clear, points-based split. Each department holds a share of a game’s revenue. Inside it, every system you build earns points by difficulty. Your points are your cut.

01

Departments hold the pool

Each department — Scripting, Visual (modeling + animation) and UI/UX — is allocated a share of the game’s revenue.

02

Work earns points

Difficulty decides reward. A small system might be ~2 points; a big, hard one ~10. The harder you carry, the more you hold.

03

Points become payout

Points convert to a real percentage of revenue (for example, ~100 points ≈ 2%). Transparent, tracked, and yours.
